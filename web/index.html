About

Get fit, make friends, have fun.

BENt On CrossFit is forging elite fitness in the Southern Highlands. We offer group classes 6 days a week as well as intensive personal training sessions available. With coaches dedicated to encouraging optimal technique and a focus on high intensity training, BENt On CrossFit is not your average gym.

No workout is the same, with people of mixed gender and ability levels all working to their own capacity, with movements tailored to individual needs.

BENt On CrossFit is also a stockist of an extensive range of supplements from Australian Sports Nutrition. With knowledgable coaches and a great deal of choice, you should be sure to find your supplementation needs. Otherwise, we can order it in!
