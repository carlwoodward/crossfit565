About

Get fit, make friends, have fun.

Your fitness is our priority at Crossfit565. Whether you're doing Crossfit to lose weight, be able play with your kids without feeling puffed or you want to be a serious athlete, you are our focus.

When you train at Crossfit565, your workouts are always varied, tailored to your level and a lot of fun. You will always feel welcome and motivated at Crossfit565.

Excellent Facilities

Our box has state of the art equipment that helps you to feel safe and secure when working out.

Tailored Programming

Every workout is scaled to meet your abilities, always pushing you forward.

Helpful Trainers

The best trainers are here to help you learn the correct techniques and increase your functional fitness.

Great Community

Make new friends in your local area. Crossfit565 is more then a gym, it's a community.

Our Facility

We have a brand new facility that caters for classes and one on one training. We have a monster rig, dedicated olympic lifting area, astroturf and the best weight lifting equipment money can buy.

Getting Started

Starting Crossfit and making fitness a priority in your life can be daunting for many people. Your enjoyment and results is our biggest priority. When you undertake our fundamentals course, you will be shown how to safely complete olympic lifts as well as get a feeling for how Crossfit works. We require all new members to complete a fundamentals course before they start so please get in touch.
